If Only I Could Read Faster
3.0 out of 5 stars Great for kids, not so much for adults.
Reviewed in the United Kingdom on April 19, 2015
Verified Purchase
I got these for me, and adult, to do the current craze of adult colouring! They come in an amazing range of colours, but some are very different from the outer colour of the pen which is frustrating. There's a yellow and green that could be used as highlighters they're so iridescent. They don't colour smoothly and if you go over a bit it makes the colour much darker which can make it difficult to get an area to look uniform in colour. They are thin so good for small details. I find the lids frustrating but they do fit in the pot (just) without you having to clip them all together. For the price they aren't bad and for kids they'd be perfect but if you're looking for a better finish then you'll need to spend a bit more!
