Revenue
Item price
Price charged to the customer. Amazon adds the item price and shipping when assessing competitiveness across sellers.
Shipping
Price charged to the customer for shipping. For seller-fulfilled orders, the seller collects this amount as revenue. For FBA orders, since shipping is already included, this amount is zero and doesn't affect FBA fulfillment fees.
Total revenue
This is the sum of Item price and Delivery
Selling on Amazon fees
To sell on the Amazon marketplace, you pay referral fees and closing fees
Fulfillment cost
Cost of seller fulfillment
Total cost per unit for you to fulfill a customers order
Fulfillment by Amazon fees
Fulfillment by Amazon fees are calculated based on the dimensions and weight of the item. The Fees Estimate assumes the product is shipped from and sold in the country where you are currently signed in to Seller Central and does not apply to the European Fulfillment Network program.
Ship to Amazon
Cost of shipping the item to an Amazon fulfillment center. You may use an average to account for zone differences.
Total fulfillment cost
This is the sum of fulfillment fees and the cost to ship to Amazon
Storage cost
Monthly storage cost per unit
The amount it costs to store a single unit of this product per month. At Amazon, this is reflected in the monthly storage fee, which is calculated based on the volume of the product.
Average inventory units stored
The average number of units you hold in inventory for this product
Storage cost per unit sold
The estimated amount you will pay in storage for the product per unit sold. Calculated by dividing the total estimated storage cost (Monthly Storage Cost per Unit X Average Inventory Units Stored) by the Estimated Monthly Units Sold for each fulfillment channel.
Your fulfillment
Amazon referral fee
Sellers pay a referral fee on each unit sold. This fee depends on the category the product belongs to and is calculated using a percentage of each unit's total price. Products in certain categories have a per-item minimum referral fee.
Variable closing fee
Variable closing fee is charged for each media item sold. All sellers pay a variable closing fee.

Price your FBA listings right  
With FBA, your Amazon Fulfillment item price can be set up to the fully landed price of your listing on other channels. FBA can help make your offers more competitive!
FBA enables your Amazon listings to qualify for many of the same shipping options and other benefits that customers expect from Amazon. For example, FBA listings qualify for Prime and Super Saver Shipping and stand out on the site with additional logos. Amazon also handles customer service and returns processing for products purchased on Amazon and fulfilled using FBA. When evaluating Fulfillment by Amazon, consider the impact of these benefits to your business.
